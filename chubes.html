Chubes

Currently developed in the Unity Game Engine using C#.

Prototype Concept

Chubes was a small game concept that revolves around the ability to craft using the materials you gather from the enviroment. His name is Chubai and he is a Chube a small cube creature that uses his resourcefullness to solve puzzles and defend himself. Making food, weapons and other objects allowed you to interact with the enviroment in different ways.
