Frontier

Personal C# Programming Learning Project

Overview

This game is a prototype concept for a first person multiplayer shooter that has an emphasis on making your own custom class and loadout on a large scale battlefield. Drive vehicles, wear armor, augment armor, mod weapons, and buy upgrades while in the match to augment your playstyle and power to dominate the enemy team. Cast Espa abilities with a magical energy source that exists in the world that can be absorbed from crystals to deal damage, support allies, stealth and protect yourself.
